VirtuMeet

Revolutionize Your Virtual Meetings with VirtuMeet!

VirtuMeet is a feature-rich video chat and meeting web app developed using WebRTC and WebSockets (socket.io). It offers seamless video calls, a robust chatbox, and intuitive screensharing capabilities. Additionally, it includes a collaborative whiteboard for real-time brainstorming and teamwork. Designed as a comprehensive Google Meet alternative, VirtuMeet ensures smooth and efficient virtual meetings, making remote collaboration effortless and effective.

Connectop

Connect with Indian Students Abroad for Unfiltered Advice!

Connectop is a platform developed with React.js that enables users from India to connect with Indian students studying abroad. Built with React.js, Node.js, and MongoDB, this platform provides essential information not typically offered by travel or educational agents. Users can access firsthand insights and advice, fostering a supportive network for prospective international students. Connectop exemplifies advanced web development skills and a commitment to enhancing the study abroad experience.
